The development of orchestral works in the late classical period

the expansion of orchestra in history started since Haydn's musical trip to London in 1791-2, the venue of his Solomom concerts in London can hold at least 800 seats, and the room size is much bigger than the concert hall in the Esterhazy palace of Eisenstadt where Haydn wrote many symphonies for and experimented symphonic form. London at that time in the eighteenth century had many concert organizations, and concert activies were active. During his service to Prince Esterhazy, he was writing ... symphonie for 14 players in which 3 violins for first and second violins, 1 viola, 1 cello, 1 double bass, 1 bassoon, 2 oboes and 2 horns. But in his symphonies for his concerts in London, he was given a condition tht allowed him to write symphonies fro between 50 to 60 players.
