Conclusion

The findings in this study are interesting. Firstly, it is found out that the Gewandhaus is actually not only referring to the concert hall or the orchestra, but also the site as well, a large building of the site Gewandhaus became adoptable after the neglect use as the storage of artillery, Gewandhaus the concert hall was built under the influence of the change in social climax of the ideas of nationaly unity, individualism.

Secondl, orchestral music development started in the late eighteenth century required bigger orchestra resulting Mendelssohn enlarged his orchestra.

Thirdly, Mendelssohn's effort in training up his Gewandhaus orchestra to become a high standard orchestra, and lastly, the composer's motivation on reviving historical repertoire was Mendelssohn's major task for educational purpose. He changed the way on how audience received from concerts, he saw this change in social aspect as his opportiunity to educate a wider audience on historical compositions. Combine all the factors that shown in above, the association between all three areas (Gewandhaus the building, its orchestra and Mendelssohn's musical contribution) is closed and interrelated.
