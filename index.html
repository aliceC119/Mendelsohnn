Gewandhaus and Felix Mendelssohn

The purpose of this study

The focus of this study will be on reseaching the association between the development of the development of the Gewandhaus the concert hall and its orchestra in Leipzig and the usical contribution given by Felix Mendelssohn during the years when he served as the Music Director in the Gewandhaus Orchester in 1835-1843 and 1845-1847.

Based on detailed relevant books and electronic infomration and interview of an art historian, present a comprehensive outook of the association between the concert hall, the orchestra and the composer.

Methods

The study will be accomplised through extenisve books and electronic source researching and interviewing an art historian and reading from his paper. The interview and his paper can give an insight of the relationship between the three areas (e.g. the concert hall, the orchestra and the composer) and able to enhance understanding of the interrelated elements (soical developmet in Europe and the development of orchetral works) that influced their association during the late eighteeth century and the early nineteenth century. During the interview, interview techniques will be ised to collect infomraiton. Interview involves asking questions to obtain specific informaiton on topic in interest.

The interview

I agreed to interview Dr. Erich Kässmayer at 6:35pm on November 6, 2012.[1] Dr. Kässmayer told me he had to catch a flight at 11:00pm on the same evening. To avoid any disturbances, I suggested conducting this intervire in L'hotel in Tsuen Wan where he was staying so he could take the airport coach nearby. I informed him about the topic I was doing and the questions I wanted to ask before he came to Hong Kong, and he gave me a written paper with information and his thoughts about my topic in interest before the interview was conducted. He was fully prepared for this interview and I wished to finished the interview without an delay within the designed time limit. Due to the difficulty to book a room for the interview, we finally found a relatively quieter area in the administrative floor to hold the interview. I bought a digital camera video function to film the interview. Extracts of the interview content will be shown in the following seclected topics in interest. Full interview clips are uploaded in Youtube.[2]
